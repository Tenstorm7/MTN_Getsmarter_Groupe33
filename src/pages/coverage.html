Network Coverage

Voice and data coverage level Implication
No coverage It is very unlikely that you will be able to establish communication,whether or outside buildings.
Limited coverage You should be able to call and text outside of buildings in most cases,but probably not inside
buildings.
Good coverage You should be able to call and text outside of buildings in most cases,and in some cases inside buildings.
Very good coverage You should be able to call and text outside of buildings, and in most cases,inside buildings.

Important information to note

1. The information on coverage is provided for information only and has no contractual value. The coverage areas are computer-simulated,so some
inaccuracles may exist.

2. Coverage may vary over time, due to many factors: season,meteorological conditions, the number of users, the appeearance of an obstacle,etc.

Terminal impact message

  • The availabity of the service can be strongly affected by the type and sensitivity of the terminal used.
